Welcome to IndoKids Playgroup


IndoKids has been established here in the heart of Zürich for over 10 years. We spend our days with the children and their lovely parents in our lovely welcoming playgroup.

Our days are spent, dancing, singing, doing art and crafts, reading stories and learning English and above all having lots of fun!

Our fabulous fully trained team ensure that every day is filled with fun and laughter here at IndoKids.

IndoKids is owned by Jidnya Mehta who has always been passionate about children education for the last 10 years, had a tremendously fun and exciting journey providing excellent standards of care and education. IndoKids has encouraged a new direction and she is looking forward to providing a positive and enriching experience to all the children that pass through IndoKids’s doors.

The Playgroup has built up an excellent reputation and we look forward to helping it grow in the future. Many of our parents and children return term after term and the purpose-built environment is welcoming and friendly.

We hope that you will come along and visit us and see what IndoKids has to offer.
